Mission

This project was inspired by a Hackathon competition when me and my team traveleled to Maine and spoke to local farmers where they brought up some of the agricultural issues and the complications that they have as we shift towards the the informational age.

Our goal is to facilitate and create a platform to connect local farmers and businesses so that it is easier for gardeners/hobbyists to receive access to better quality and more inexpensive products no matter where they are located, all while supporting and fostering relationships between smaller vendors.

From 2011 to 2016 organic farming has risen by 56% ($7.6 billion) , yet still making up a very small share of U.S. farmland, a total of 1%. Despite this, domestic supply in organic agriculture has been increasing rapidly and demand for other agricultural products is increasing even quicker (20% annually). Currently, one can only find standard types of seeds or limited with their selection of choice when purchasing vegetable/fruit seeds, hindering for an organic or non-GMO cultivation. Even If one can find local distributors for these products, they are not always held in a quantity that meets one's demands, especially farmers. Similarly, for many gardeners and hobbyists one of the problems they face with is the accessibility in acquiring seeds from different zones. Many stores only provide only seeds that are optimal for that specific zone or region, but with the increase of greenhouse farming many can plant and sustain vegitation from different zones. Even though one may find many of these seeds online, most sites do not have a well maintained and categorized inventory to find them based on personalized needs This forces many to resort to other large corporations and distributors such as Monsanto. At a larger scale, when farmers want to find a seed based on how long they will last, the hybrid status of the seed, and the quality of the seed, there is no way to filter that based on personal specifications leading to uncertainty in quality online.

Solution

Seedbase. Our search engine and tool based service providing local farmers, businesses, and garden enthusiasts an efficient solution to finding, comparing, and filtering criteria that gardeners account for when planting seeds, such as price, hybrid status, and much more - offering a wide variety of products of vegetation and flowers. Using a organized compilation of passionate local businesses and farms offering more accessibility to fresher and cheaper products. More than just offers multiple search options and filters allowing you find a product based on your needs. Our backend utilizes elasticsearch in order to enhance and provide a sophisticated search engine for products while using Kibana in order to recieve realtime data updates and analytics on what customers are most interested in, gathering information regarding agricultural demands to cater to individuals needs.

Mission

The Unified Ceilometer Network (UCN) is a collaboration between the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC), the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) and National Atmospheric and Oceanic Administration (NOAA) on a ground-based ceilometer network to support activities that will provide a comprehensive three-dimensional assessment of the chemical and dynamical processes in the lower atmosphere by coupling surface measurements, ceilometer and satellite observations that can aid future policy decisions and strategies to key questions on the influence of gases and aerosols in air quality, atmospheric composition and climate.

Solution

As a technical lead, my role was to devise a format to standardize cloud data coming from sites in and out of the country to a NetCDF based format to represent three-dimensional data and recieve a better understanding of the atmosphere. Aside from the I archecturally designed and implemented applications and backend systems to recieve and process data from 30+ sites in real time from all around the North America region. Apart from that, I oversaw the process and developed an application to dynamically generate realtime LiDAR visualizations with other developers and communicated with Agencies and researchers to understand and devise solutions to build the application to around their needs.
